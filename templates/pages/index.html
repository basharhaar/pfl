Game Format

The format of the game will be 5V5. Eight players will be in te squad and five player in field. Professional Referees will be officaiating the matches. There will be 20 teams divided into 4 groups.

Time Format

The game will be divided into 4 quaters, 8 minutes each. 1 minute break time between 1st and 3rd quater. 5 minutes break during the half time.
